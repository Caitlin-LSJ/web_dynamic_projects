But that was life

19 Nov 2020, Thursday

"But that was life: Nobody got a guided tour to their own theme park. You had to hop on the rides as they presented themselves, never knowing whether you would like the one you were in line for... or if the bastard was going to make you throw up your corn dog and your cotton candy all over the place."

- J.R. Ward, Crave

Why do you keep Dead Roses?

22 Nov 2020, Sunday

A fresh Rose is smelled for its scent but a dead one - for the memories. A fresh Rose gives the feel of softness but a dead one gives the feel of past. A fresh Rose lives for days but a dead one

- Forever."
